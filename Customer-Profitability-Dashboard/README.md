****Customer Profitability & Retention Insights****

This dashboard analyzes customer sales performance, profitability, and retention patterns.

**Key Features:**

Sales and Profit KPIs

Customer retention and churn analysis

Customer segmentation by profitability

Region-wise sales performance

Product category profit analysis

**Business Insights:**

Retained customers generate higher profits.

Regional sales trends help identify high-performing markets.

Customer segmentation helps improve targeted marketing strategies.
